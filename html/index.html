MoSKito Counter

MoSKito Counter is a small application the allows us to track the usage of MoSKito Tools all over the world. Its sole purpose is to give us, the developers, the positive feedback of seeing people actually using the framework.

MoSKito counter gathers NO personal or other information, its sole purpose is to count the requests to WebUI and Control. You can see worldwide requests to MoSKito here:
